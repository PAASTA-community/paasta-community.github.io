### PAASTA Bioinformatics Summer School 🤖💻☀️
PAASTA is hosting a bioinformatics-focused palaeoproteomics summer school (online) in June.
The summer school will contain both theory and practical exercises on a variety of palaeoproteomic analyses, including ZooMS, amelogenin sex estimation and shotgun proteomics.
The summer school is free and open to all levels of experience.<br> 
Practical details see below ⬇️ 

### Practical details
- Date: 08-06-2026 to 12-06-2026
- Location: Online
- Application deadline: 01-06-2026 [**Sign-up form**](https://forms.gle/pqmecUpD7Xayv3Af6) | [**Programme**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1tJS0FlNSwCZ_uRiNyTe_AnOrtr6KAVLW3yTGlnF2E34/edit?gid=0#gid=0)

All sessions will be recorded and shared on the [PAASTA YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@PAASTAcommunity/videos) after the summer school.
