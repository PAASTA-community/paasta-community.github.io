### PaleoProPhyler: a reproducible pipeline for phylogenetic inference using ancient proteins.
In this talk, Ioannis Patramanis presents his new tool for protein-based phylogenetic analysis and the associated opportunities and challenges.

A recording of this talk is available on [PAASTA's YouTube channel](https://youtu.be/2gVEQyinL4A?si=w-QvBtbAqF9kTL8N).
