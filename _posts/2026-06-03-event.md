### Assessing the potential of ancient protein sequences in the study of hominid evolution
This month, our speaker is Ioannis Patramanis from the University of Copenhagen, who will be discussing how ancient protein sequences contribute to the study of hominid evolution.

Join live via [Zoom](https://ucph-ku.zoom.us/j/69173455929?pwd=CMA03VKpAnprYXcqxFmt3iQL1x7VsG.1), or watch later on [PAASTA's YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@PAASTAcommunity).
