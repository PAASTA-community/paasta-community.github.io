### Elephant Ecology in a Neanderthal Landscape
In this talk, Elena Armaroli (UNIMORE) will be discussing the life histories of straight-tusked elephants from Neumark-Nord (~125 ka, Germany).

Join live via [Zoom](https://ucph-ku.zoom.us/j/65245894718?pwd=1sFJHg8FKpapc2SAIxz1o8IEQaXOC9.1), or watch later on [PAASTA's YouTube channel](https://youtu.be/sejUDEL2MZE?si=XOfeG97scAN_B0lC).
